Virtual Existence

Timeline of technological developments in video games

1993

In 1993, the first Doom marked the debut of 3D, representing at the time the pinnacle of videogame realism.

In 2009, Assassin's Creed 2 revolutionized the video game market with advanced 3D and highly realistic NPCs capable of reacting to the player's actions.

2009

2020

In 2020, Cyberpunk 2077 transforms video gaming by offering characters to the point of seeming alive and emotional.

Released in 2023, Unrecord is universally recognized as the most realistic game ever made. Everything about it is incredibly faithful to reality, from the physical effects to the visuals and sounds. effects, visuals and sounds.

Meta Quest 3, launched in 2023, has redefined our approach to virtual reality. It integrates augmented reality, radically transforming our perception of the world.

2023

2077

In 2077, we have the ability to create worlds that become true alternative realities. It would therefore be possible to live entirely in a virtual universe, escaping physical death and offering the illusion of immortality.
